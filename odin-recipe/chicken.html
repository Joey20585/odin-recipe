Fried chicken is a crispy, flavorful dish made by marinating chicken in buttermilk and spices,
then coating it in seasoned flour. The chicken is fried in hot oil until golden brown and cooked through
, creating a crunchy crust and juicy interior. Perfectly paired with sides like coleslaw or mashed potatoes, it's a comforting
and delicious treat.

INGREDIENTS

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying

STEPS

    STEP 1

    Place crackers in a large resealable plastic bag; seal bag and crush crackers with
    a rolling pin until they are coarse crumbs. Add the flour, potato flakes, seasoned
    salt, and pepper and mix well.

    STEP 2

    Beat egg in a shallow dish or bowl. One by one, dredge chicken pieces in egg,
    then place in bag with crumb mixture. Seal bag and shake to coat

    STEP 3

    Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

    STEP 4

    Fry chicken, turning frequently, until golden brown and juices run clear, 15 to
    20 minutes.
